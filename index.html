What is Sports Management?

SHOW ME THE MONEY!!!-Jerry Maguire

Every time I am asked what my major is in college, I reply with "Sports Management," knowing that I will have to follow up with details. Not many know what Sports Management is in the context of a major, and even less know what job fields it leads to. Here is a closer look and what it means to be in Sports Management, the requirements you must fufill, and the opportunities it can lead to.

What it's all about

Sports Management itself is a very broad major. Majoring in Sports Management can take you anywhere from a High School P.E. Coach, to a Sports Agent, to the Athletic Director of a University. A degree in Sports Management is focused on preparing students to have successful careers in every branch of athletics. Sports Management degrees can be combined with degrees in other fields, such as business, health or law, and can lead you into a career in the billion dollar industry that is sports.

Now What?

Okay so let's say you have your degree, Congratulations! But what is the point of a degree if you don't know what you can do with it. As I said before, Sports Management is a broad major, and because of that can it can lead to many job opportunities in multiple fields. With a Sports Management degree, careers can be found in a business setting, or a more hands on setting. Here are merely some of the careers that a degree in Sports Management can open up doors for:

Athletic Trainor: health care professionals who provide emergency, therapeutic, and rehabilitation services to athletes and injuries

College Athletic Administrator: sometimes referred to an Athletic Director; supervises the work of coaches and other staff in various athletic programs

Sports Agent: seeks out and negotiates sponsorship and employment contracts for various athletes. Agents act as representatives for athletes in their professional career

Operations Manager: oversees the production of goods and services in various sports settings

Athletic Team Manager: handle day to day operations, from business to financial, of an entire sports team

Ticket Sales Specialist: oversees the sale and marketing of tickets to events
